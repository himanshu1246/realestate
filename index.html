About The Developer & Project

A. R. Constructions'
Vrundavan

A. R. Constructions is a premier construction company dedicated to transforming urban landscapes with innovative and sustainable architectural designs. True to our motto, "We build everlasting relations...", we are proud to present Vrundavan.

Vrundavan is a spectacular G+21 Storey Residential-cum-Commercial development located strategically in Sector 6, Pethali, Taloja One, Navi Mumbai 410208. Designed for modern families, Vrundavan flawlessly blends quality construction, luxury, and day-to-day retail convenience.
